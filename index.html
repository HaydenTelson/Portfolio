DropBy

Description:
DropBy is an app for college students to discover and post events that are happening on campus. It aims to help spread the word about activities happening on a specific college campus in the hopes of getting students more engaged with campus life. It additionally helps new students find activities in which they can meet new people. Through the app, students can find nearby events, filter by event type, or post events they are involved with so that other students can see them. Each posting contains information about the events so that attendees know exactly what to expect when they Drop By.
Roles:
Project Manager, Designer, Software Developer
